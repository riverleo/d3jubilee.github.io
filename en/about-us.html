Next-Gen Capital

Seoul and San Francisco-based D3Jubilee, established in July 2011, is uniquely positioned for global impact investing. We redefine the role of capital by bringing entrepreneurial innovation to make our society socially and environmentally sustainable, and improve the lives of those most in need such as low-income families, immigrants and women.

What We Do

Global Investing

We identify and invest in ICT-based startups of high socio-economic value across advanced and underdeveloped countries (e.g., South Korea, USA, India, countries in South America and Africa).

Sector Building

To build and cultivate the impact-investing ecosystem in Korea, we provide opportunities to educate and activate investors through educational sessions and conferences, and collaborate with other investors in D3's global networks.

Investment Strategies

(1) Thematic problem-solving

We select the specific theme by investment sectors, where social or environmental needs offer commercial growth opportunities and invest in companies solving those themes.

- Education: Adaptive learning, Affordable learning, Content & tools for 'Left-Behind'
- Healthcare: Dementia, Mental disease, Affordable device, Accessibility to patient data
- Inclusive finance, Energy access, Supply chain, Food security, etc.

(2) Holistic system approach

We improve the competitive power of business model by encouraging collaboration among our portfolio companies.
