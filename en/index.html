D3 Jubilee

D3Jubilee means a stepping stone and systematic change to enable the underprivileged to gain their feet and shape better future.

  • D3: A stepping stone (*3 Ds are derived from the Korean word, “Didimdol” meaning a stepping stone)
  • Jubilee: Communal joy within a community where disadvantaged people are given ample opportunities for shaping better future
